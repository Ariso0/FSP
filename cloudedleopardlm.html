Clouded leopard

Description

Clouded leopards are one of the only cats in the world that can climb down, hang upside down from and hunt in trees. However, despite their ability to u lize trees for hiding and hun ng, and having a coat with cloud-like pa erns that help blend these cats into their environment, clouded leopards are on the decline.

information

Sunda clouded leopards get their name from “Sundaland” or the “Sunda region” which refers to the islands of Sumatra, Borneo, Java, Bali and the Malayan Peninsula where the cats can be found. However, despite being coated in a pa ern that helps them blend into the habitat of the region where they live, they are unprotected against the consistent habitat loss that occurs in the Sunda region. Global Conserva on works relessly to protect the Sunda clouded leopard by equipping rangers with SMART technology and necessary equipment that help them more effec vely patrol the regions that are home to this vulnerable species.
